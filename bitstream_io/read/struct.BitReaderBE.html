fn new(reader: &mut BufRead) -> BitReaderBE

Wraps a big-endian reader around a BufRead reference.

A BufRead is required because this reader is liable to make many small reads to the stream in normal operation, so reading from the buffer directly is preferable to making many calls to read_exact.

Skips the given number of bits in the stream. Since this method does not need an accumulator, it may be slightly faster than reading to an empty variable.
